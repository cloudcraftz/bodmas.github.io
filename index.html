How to Play

Guess the BODMAS in 3 tries.
Each guess must be a valid three numbers(0 - 9) and two operators(+, -, *, /). Hit the enter button to submit.
After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the equation.
Examples

2

The number 2 is in the equation and in the correct spot.

5

The number 5 is in the equation but in the wrong spot.

9

The number 9 is not in the equation in any spot.

A new equation will be available after completing the current equation.
